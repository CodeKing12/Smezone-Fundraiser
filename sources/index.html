Aim of this partnership

This communication seeks to engage our DBALite customers as regards giving them access to investors by selling a paid investor readiness program to them which after taking the course, gives them access to pitch to investors after completion of the paid courses and submission of pitch proposal.

About GetFundedAfrica

The GetFunded Africa’s Investor Readiness Program is an online, pre-recorded, 4-week course that teaches African businesses how to raise venture finance of between $100k to $12m from global investors. The program provides practical skills to navigate the funding process, determine valuations, create financial projections & develop a fundraising pitch deck.
